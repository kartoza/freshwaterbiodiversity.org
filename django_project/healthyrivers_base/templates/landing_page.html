Human needs, conservation, and water resources collide in South Africa where threats to freshwater biodiversity and ecosystem services will only increase in the years ahead.
Freshwater ecosystems including rivers, lakes and wetlands not only support a wealth of biodiversity, but they are also fundamental to our economic, social and spiritual well-being.
Water is the most critical resource issue of our lifetime and our children's lifetime. The health of our rivers is the principal measure of how we live on the land.
